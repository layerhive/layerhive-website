FAQ

Common questions before ordering.

Can I send an STL file?

Yes. Send your STL file and we will review if it is printable, estimate print time and send a quote.

Can you help if I only have an idea?

Yes. Describe what you need and we can discuss the next step. Complex 3D design work may be quoted separately.

How fast can you print?

Timing depends on size, detail, quantity and current workload. Small projects can often be handled quickly after approval.

Do you ship orders?

Yes. Pickup and shipping options can be discussed after the quote.
